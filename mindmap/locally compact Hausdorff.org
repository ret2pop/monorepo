* Introduction
A locally compact Hausdorff space is a Hausdorff space in which every point has a [[id:e4ac2e89-1975-40de-9d6a-98281a3ca83e][compact neighbourhood]]. In particular, the [[id:339b32e7-ad89-40d7-8b11-5b293bd1056f][One Point Compactification]]
of a locally compact Hausdorff space is compact Hausdorff, so they are interesting to study for that reason.
